About CodeFest Anambra 1.0

CodeFest Anambra 1.0 is Anambra’s leading event for coding and
creative problem-solving, Our mission is to bring together the region's
top tech talent, students, and entrepreneurs in an inspiring environment
to foster growth, collaboration, and the showcasing of skills. Through
this platform, we aim to strengthen Anambra's tech ecosystem and
empower the next generation of innovators.
